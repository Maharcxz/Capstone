AR Glasses Try-On QR Code Generator

Generate a QR code to easily access the AR Glasses Try-On application on mobile devices.

How to use:

  1. Enter the URL where your AR Glasses Try-On application is hosted
  2. Click "Generate QR Code"
  3. Scan the QR code with your mobile device
  4. Allow camera permissions when prompted
  5. Position your face in the center of the screen
  6. Use the dropdown to try different glasses styles

Note: For the best experience, use a modern mobile browser like Chrome or Safari on a device with a front-facing camera.
